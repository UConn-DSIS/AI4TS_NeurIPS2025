AI4TS: NeurIPS'25 Workshop




AI for Time Series Workshop


From Multimodal to Agentic Time Series Analysis

The AI for Time Series (AI4TS) Workshop at NeurIPS 2025 focuses on the latest advances and challenges in time series analysis, with a special emphasis on multimodal learning and agentic intelligence. The workshop aims to bring together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to discuss new methods, benchmarks, and applications for multimodal and agentic time series data. We welcome contributions on a broad range of topics, including but not limited to forecasting, fusion, reasoning, anomaly detection, reinforcement learning, decision-making, and real-world applications in healthcare, finance, energy, transportation, and more.

Call for Papers: Topics of Interest

This workshop welcomes submissions on a broad range of topics related to time series analysis, multimodal learning, and agentic intelligence. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Multimodal Time Series Analysis:
    • Multimodal time series forecasting and prediction
    • Multimodal fusion of time series with text, images, and graphs
    • Knowledge-grounded time series reasoning (e.g., clinical notes + vitals)
    • Spatio-temporal forecasting
    • Multimodal time series anomaly detection and diagnosis
    • Representation learning for multimodal time series
    • Interpretation and explanation in multimodal time series
    • Cross-modal alignment and representation learning
    • Temporal question answering over multimodal time series data
    • Interactive and embodied time series agents
    • Vision-language-time models for real-world monitoring systems
  • Agentic AI for (Multimodal) Time Series:
    • Reinforcement learning with temporal signals
    • Decision-making and planning in (multimodal) time series systems
    • Model-based control and adaptive feedback mechanisms
    • Multi-agent systems and coordination with temporal models
    • Causal reasoning and counterfactual analysis in (multimodal) time series
    • Agent behavior modeling from (multimodal) temporal data
  • Benchmarks, Systems, and Applications:
    • Benchmark datasets and evaluation protocols for multimodal/agentic time series data
    • Interpretability of deployed multimodal/agentic time series models
    • Multimodal/Agentic time series applications in Healthcare, finance, energy systems and smart grids, transportation, earth science, agriculture, and climate modeling

Submission

For the paper track, the submission should be 4-8 pages long with the main context following the NeurIPS 2025 template. We especially welcome new ideas with preliminary results in multimodal and agentic time series analysis. The submission website will be set up via the OpenReview platform. Each paper will be reviewed by 3 reviewers and a meta-reviewer will make the final decision. COI rules will be applied to both chairs and reviewers. Authors of selected accepted papers will be invited to give a 15-minute oral presentation. All accepted papers will be invited to give poster presentations during the poster session.

Awards

Supported by Squirrel Ai Learning, we will set up Awards to acknowledge high-quality accepted workshop papers, including 1 Best Paper Award, 1 Best Paper Runner-up Award, and 1 Best Paper Honorable Mention Award. For the three awards, each awardee will receive a monetary prize, in addition to a certificate.
